Codec64 Guide

A free online tool that lets you securely encode, restore, and convert images locally inside your browser.

❓ What is Codec64?

Codec64 is an image encoder and decoder that allows you to:

  • Convert image to Base64 (internally, without showing complexity)
  • Turn images into private downloadable files
  • Secure images with password protection
  • Restore images instantly
  • Convert images between formats

It works completely online without requiring any login.

🖼️ How to Encode an Image Online

Encoding an image means turning it into a portable and private format that can be stored or shared easily.

🔄 How to Decode and Restore Image

To restore your image:

1

Upload the encoded image

Upload the PNG image payload you previously generated.

2

Enter password & recover

Input your password (if used) and instantly recover the original picture.

🔒 Secure Image Sharing

Codec64 allows secure image sharing without uploading your data anywhere.

  • Share encoded image files safely
  • Add password protection for extra security
  • Only authorized users can restore the image

⚡ Image to Base64 Converter

Internally, the app converts your image into a format similar to Base64, allowing it to be stored and restored perfectly.

You don’t need to understand technical details — everything works automatically.

🔁 Convert Image Format Online

You can also use Codec64 as an image converter:

  • Convert image to PNG
  • Convert image to JPEG
  • Convert image to WEBP

This works instantly in your browser.

🛡️ Your Privacy Matters

Codec64 is designed as a privacy-first image tool.

  • No image uploads to servers
  • No tracking of your files
  • No storage of your data
  • Everything runs locally in your browser

Your images remain completely under your control.

⚠️ Why Does the Encoded Image Look Different?

The encoded image may look blank or dark because it stores hidden data instead of a visible picture.

This is expected and ensures your image is stored securely.

Frequently Asked Questions
